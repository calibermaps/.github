# CaliberMaps

CaliberMaps is the GIS research and development division of
**[Motherlode Prospecting LLC](https://www.motherlodeprospecting.com)** — the
skunkworks where our engineering happens, and where **VectorTopo** grew from an
internal field tool into a soon-to-be public product.

VectorTopo is a field-first mapping platform for land, claims, terrain, and the
outdoors. It brings authoritative public data into one fast map — mining claims,
public land and parcels, hunting and fishing regulations, geology, backcountry,
weather, and high-resolution terrain — on the web and on iOS and Android.

## Open source

VectorTopo stands on the open-source geospatial community, and we are committed
to giving back as much as we take — contributing fixes, tools, and improvements
upstream wherever we can.
